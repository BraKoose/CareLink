## 🎯 TODO 1: Problem Statement

### What is the Problem?
Over **15,000 Ghanaians acquire HIV annually**, including **4,732 young people aged 15–24**—representing over 30% of all new infections. Lifesaving medicines like Antiretrovirals (ARVs), Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis (PrEP), Post-Exposure Prophylaxis (PEP), and home self-test kits are widely available and medically effective. 

However, **there is a severe collapse of trust** in traditional delivery systems. The actual barrier to treatment is not medicine unavailability; it is **extreme social stigma, public discrimination, fear of being recognized at physical clinics, and lack of confidential channels.**

### Who is Affected?
- **Primary ICP ("Elvis", Age 27):** Lives with HIV, is digitally active, but frequently skips medication doses and clinic visits due to intense fear of facing neighbors, family, or coworkers at public healthcare centers.
- **Secondary ICPs:**
  - Young people (15–35) seeking confidential sexual and reproductive health (SRH) guidance.
  - Partners of HIV-positive individuals who need daily PrEP support and private testing.
  - Highly marginalized groups (sex workers, MSM, adolescents) facing societal backlash.

### Why Does This Matter?
Delaying testing and skipping treatment doses doesn't just impact individual health—it leads to:
1. **Treatment Failure & Drug Resistance:** Skipping daily doses causes the virus to adapt, making standard regimens (like TLD) ineffective.
2. **Viral Load Spikes:** Higher viral load increases the probability of transmission.
3. **Preventable Deaths:** Young lives are lost due to a lack of a safe, non-judgmental environment.

## 🚀 TODO 2: Proposed Solution

CareLink solves this crisis by introducing **stigma-resistant, trust-based digital health navigation**. It transforms how users receive guidance, access medication, and sustain daily adherence in complete confidentiality.

### Four Pillars of CareLink

#### 1. AI Health Navigator
A 24/7 empathetic companion that answers sensitive health questions. It provides clear, clinical info on HIV education, PrEP/PEP usage, side effects, and mental health support, eliminating the fear of judgment.

#### 2. Private Care Marketplace
An anonymous shop where users can order over-the-counter STI self-test kits, condoms, lubricants, or upload a prescription to buy ARVs/PrEP.

#### 3. Adherence Engine
An offline-first checklist featuring:
- Confidential daily pill logging with gamified streak tracking.
- Secure, customizable refill alarms ("Daily Dose" or "Clinic Refill") that do not expose health details on lock screens.

#### 4. Privacy Infrastructure (Stigma-Resistant Design)
- **Zero Real Names / GPS Logs:** Accounts are managed using a automatically generated pseudonymous username and anonymous cryptographic IDs.
- **Discreet Delivery Disguise Packaging:** Medication orders are delivered inside everyday items chosen by the user. Options include:
  - 📦 **Standard Unbranded Box (Free):** Plain cardboard container with security tape and zero labels.
  - 🍕 **Pizza Box Disguise (GHS 5.00):** Concealed inside a standard local pizza box to mimic a lunch delivery.
  - 🍗 **Warm Jollof Rice Lunchbox (GHS 12.00):** Disguised inside a fresh warm meal delivery, blending in completely.
  - 🥣 **Nutritious Milo & Oats Pack (GHS 15.00):** Packed alongside daily recovery nutrients, reinforcing strength and immune support.
  - 🍊 **Fresh Fruit & Orange Crate (GHS 18.00):** Delivered with organic local fruits to support medication adherence and health.
  - 🥜 **High-Protein Cashew & Shake (GHS 14.00):** Enclosed with immune-strengthening dietary products.

## 🤖 TODO 3: How AI Is Used

We use AI meaningfully to act as a **24/7 Personal HIV Care Companion**, guiding patients through prevention, treatment, and daily adherence.

### Core AI Architecture & Details
- **Model Used:** Google Gemini (Generative AI API).
- **Clinical Grounding (RAG-ready):** The conversational model is strictly prompted and grounded on **Ghana Health Service (GHS) & WHO Clinical Guidelines**. This completely mitigates hallucinations and guarantees clinical credibility.
- **Empathetic Navigation:** Rather than dry, medical jargon, the AI acts with professional warmth, comforting users experiencing acute mental distress or crisis.
- **Adherence Recovery Plan:** When a user logs a missed medication dose on their checklist, the AI companion proactively formulates a supportive recovery sequence (e.g., advising on delayed intake windows and offering encouragement) instead of warning or shaming.

## 🛠️ TODO 4: Installation & Setup

CareLink is built entirely using **Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, and Material Design 3 (M3)** with a local Room Database for offline storage.

### Prerequisites
- Android Studio Ladybug (or newer).
- JDK 17.
- An Android Emulator or physical device (API level 26+).

### Step-by-Step Installation

1. **Clone the Repository:**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/godfredamoako/carelink.git
   cd carelink
   ```

2. **Configure Environment Secrets:**
   Create a `.env` file in the root directory (using `.env.example` as a guide) and add your Gemini API key:
   ```env
   GEMINI_API_KEY=your_google_gemini_api_key_here
   ```

3. **Build the Project:**
   Open the root directory in Android Studio. Wait for the Gradle sync to finish.

4. **Run the App:**
   Select your device/emulator and click the **Run** button (or press `Shift + F10`).
